Who We Are

The Heart of InAmigos

We are a youth-led organization committed to bringing positive change in society through various grassroots initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood opportunities. We strive to create an inclusive environment where every individual can thrive and contribute to society's overall progress.

  • Empowering youth
  • Sustainable development
  • Community building

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their background, has equal opportunities to achieve their full potential. We envision a society that is compassionate, environmentally conscious, and driven by the youth to solve tomorrow's challenges today.

  • Equal opportunities
  • Environmental awareness
  • Compassionate society

Our Initiatives

Projects That Matter

Discover our six flagship programs designed to address critical social and environmental challenges across communities.

SEVA

Providing food, clothes, and essential supplies to marginalized communities and homeless individuals.

BACHPANSHALA

Ensuring foundational education, holistic care, and developmental support for underprivileged children.

JEEV

Dedicated to animal welfare, rescue operations, medical treatment, and finding forever homes for strays.

UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship programs.

PRAKRITI

Protecting our environment via massive plantation drives, waste management, and awareness campaigns.

VIKAS

Fostering youth development, leadership skills, and providing transformative internship opportunities.
